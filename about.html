Me


Jaleesa Rosario is an Icelandic Born Afro Caribbean American modern dancer and mixed media motion artist. Throughout her artistic career she has operated in every form of expression from drawing to welding, however she has found her passion in the combination of artistic expression and technological manipulation. She has been featured in various media forms across multiple platforms like an artist feature for Amy Poehlers Smart Girls, entertainment for San Francisco GLBT Historical Gala, and the development designer for the Brooklyn Museum.


As a experience designer for human computer interactions and events, Jaleesa has leveraged her passion for creative design thinking to support her career in technology, as a information architect and UX Researcher studying at the University of Michigan at the School of Information.


She specializes in creative innovation, education, diversity and inclusion development within artificial intelligence training, experience design, and human computer interaction. Her goal is to develop better experiences by incorporating creative mixed methods research, development, and design. Forever dedicated to the creation of new information initiatives and tools aimed to advance the art/science of information.
